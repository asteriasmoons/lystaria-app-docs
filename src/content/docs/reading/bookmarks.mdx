## Bookmark folders
When you open the Bookmarks page, the first card you see allows you to create new bookmark folders. Folders are the foundation of how your bookmarks are organized, giving you a way to separate links by topic, purpose, or interest.

<div class="docs-image-row">
<img src="/img/screenshots/bookmarks/view.png" class="docs-image-small"/>
<img src="/img/screenshots/bookmarks/addfolder.png" class="docs-image-small"/>
</div>

Each folder can be customized with a name and an icon. You can choose from a large library of icons, allowing you to visually represent what that folder is for. This makes it easier to recognize your folders at a glance and gives your bookmark system a more personal feel.

<DotDivider />

## Saving links with the share sheet
One of the easiest ways to add bookmarks is through the share sheet. When you are browsing in your browser, you can tap the share icon and select Lystaria to save the link directly into your bookmarks.

<div class="docs-image-row">
<img src="/img/screenshots/bookmarks/share1.png" class="docs-image-small"/>
<img src="/img/screenshots/bookmarks/share2.png" class="docs-image-small"/>
</div>

This makes saving links feel seamless. Instead of copying and pasting URLs manually, you can send them straight into your chosen folder with minimal effort. It turns bookmarking into something that fits naturally into how you already browse.

<DotDivider />

## Adding bookmarks manually
In addition to using the share sheet, you can also add bookmarks directly inside the app. When you open a folder, you will see a plus icon in the header. Tapping this opens the bookmark creation view.

<img src="/img/screenshots/bookmarks/folderview.png" class="docs-image-single"/>

From here, you can enter all the details for your bookmark. This includes the title, description, the link itself, and any tags you want to assign. You can also choose which folder the bookmark belongs to before saving. Once everything is filled out, simply save the bookmark and it will appear inside your selected folder.

<div class="docs-image-row">
<img src="/img/screenshots/bookmarks/add1.png" class="docs-image-small"/>
<img src="/img/screenshots/bookmarks/add2.png" class="docs-image-small"/>
</div>

This method gives you more control over how your bookmarks are organized. It is especially useful when you want to add extra context, categorize your links properly, or save something that was not captured through the share sheet.

<DotDivider />

## Favoriting bookmarks
Inside a bookmark, you can mark important links as favorites by tapping the star icon on the card. This gives you a quick way to highlight the links that matter most to you.

Favoriting helps separate everyday links from the ones you return to often. Over time, this makes it easier to focus on what is most useful without needing to search through everything you have saved.

<DotDivider />

## Bookmark card actions
When you open a bookmark card, you are given several actions for managing that link.

You can move the bookmark to another folder, open it directly in Safari, or delete it if you no longer need it. These actions are placed at the top of the card so they are always easy to access without navigating away.

This keeps interaction simple and consistent. Everything you might want to do with a bookmark is available in one place.

<DotDivider />

## Notes and live preview
Below the bookmark actions, you will find a notes section and a live preview of the link.

<div class="docs-image-row">
<img src="/img/screenshots/bookmarks/markview1.png" class="docs-image-small"/>
<img src="/img/screenshots/bookmarks/markview2.png" class="docs-image-small"/>
</div>

The notes box allows you to write anything you want about the bookmark. This could be a reminder of why you saved it, key points from the page, or anything else that gives the link more meaning to you. It turns a simple URL into something more contextual and personal.

The live preview allows you to view the content of the link directly within the app. Instead of leaving Lystaria to check what the link contains, you can preview it instantly. This makes it easier to revisit saved content and quickly decide what you want to engage with.

<GradientDivider />

The Bookmarks feature in Lystaria is designed to be simple on the surface but meaningful in use. By combining folders, favorites, notes, and previews, it creates a system where your saved links are not just stored, but actually organized and accessible in a way that supports how you think and explore.

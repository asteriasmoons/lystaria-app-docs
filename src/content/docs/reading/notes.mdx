  <h2>Introduction</h2>
  <p>

## Organizing with tabs
Notes can be grouped into different tabs so you can separate your thoughts by category if you want to.

<img src="/img/screenshots/notes/tab.png" class="docs-image-single"/>

You can create a new tab using the icon in the page header. Once created, you can rename or delete a tab by long pressing it to open the context menu. This keeps tab management simple while still giving you flexibility in how you organize your notes.

<DotDivider />

## Creating notes
You can create a new note using the floating plus button on the screen. When creating a note, you can choose its color, add up to two labels, and decide whether you want to pin it.

<div class="docs-image-row">
<img src="/img/screenshots/notes/new1.png" class="docs-image-single"/>
<img src="/img/screenshots/notes/new2.png" class="docs-image-single"/>
</div>

These small options allow each note to carry just enough context without becoming complicated. Colors help with visual grouping, while labels give you quick identifiers for what the note is about.

<DotDivider />

## Voice input
Notes can also be created using your voice. Inside a newly created note, you can tap the microphone icon to begin speaking.

Your speech is transcribed and inserted directly into the note, allowing you to capture thoughts without typing. This makes it easier to record ideas in the moment, especially when you do not want to stop and write everything out manually.

<DotDivider />

## Pinned notes
Pinned notes appear at the top of your list so you can keep important thoughts within easy reach.

<img src="/img/screenshots/notes/view.png" class="docs-image-single"/>

You can collapse pinned notes by tapping the arrow on the sticky note card next to the pin icon. This allows you to keep them accessible without taking up too much space on the screen.

<DotDivider />

## Viewing your notes
Six notes are displayed at a time to keep the interface clean and manageable. If you have more notes, a load more button appears at the bottom of the list so you can reveal additional entries when needed.

This approach prevents the page from feeling cluttered while still allowing you to access everything.

<DotDivider />

## Note details
Tapping a sticky note card opens a full view of that note. This allows you to read its contents without being limited by the preview size.

<img src="/img/screenshots/notes/preview.png" class="docs-image-single"/>

From this view, you can also edit or delete the note. Keeping these actions inside the full view makes it easier to manage notes without adding extra controls to the main list.

<GradientDivider />

The Notes feature in Lystaria is designed to stay simple, fast, and flexible. With tabs, colors, labels, pinning, and voice input, it gives you just enough structure to stay organized while still letting your thoughts flow naturally.

## Announcements Board
You can start or join a buddy read through the global announcements board. This is where users post about books they are currently reading and invite others to join them.

</div>

When you see a book you are interested in, you can join that buddy read and become part of the group. This makes it easy to find others who are reading the same book without needing to search or coordinate outside of the app.

<DotDivider />

## Group Chat
Once you join a buddy read, you gain access to a shared chat with everyone in the group.

This chat allows you to communicate freely with your reading buddies. You can discuss the story, share reactions, ask questions, or simply talk about what you are experiencing as you read. It brings a social layer into reading that helps keep you connected and engaged.

<DotDivider />

## Progress Updates
Inside the chat, you can update your reading progress using the progress button. This allows you to quickly share what page or chapter you are currently on.

These updates help everyone in the group stay aware of each other’s progress. It makes it easier to avoid spoilers, stay in sync when needed, and understand where everyone is in the book without having to constantly ask.

<GradientDivider />

Buddy Reading adds a shared dimension to the Reading system in Lystaria. By combining discovery, communication, and progress tracking, it creates an experience where reading becomes something you can enjoy alongside others instead of entirely on your own.

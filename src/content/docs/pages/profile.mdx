## Profile setup
You can personalize your profile by uploading a profile picture. This gives your account a visual identity and makes the app feel more like your own space.

You can also configure your time zone here. Setting the correct time zone ensures that reminders, events, and time-based features behave accurately based on where you are.

<DotDivider />

## Account actions
The Profile page includes the option to sign out of your account. This allows you to securely log out whenever needed.

<img src="/img/screenshots/profile/view1.png" class="docs-image-single"/>

Keeping this action here makes it easy to access without placing it in multiple locations throughout the app.

<DotDivider />

## Calendar sync
Calendar sync allows Lystaria to connect with your device’s calendar system.

You can turn sync on or off at any time. When enabled, you can choose which calendars to sync from and which calendars to sync to. This gives you full control over how your events interact with external calendars.

Because the sync is two-way, changes made in Lystaria can reflect in your selected calendar, and events from that calendar can also appear within Lystaria.

<DotDivider />

## Managing calendars
You can manage your calendars directly from the Profile page. This includes editing the calendar name and color, or deleting the calendar entirely.

This keeps your calendar system flexible, allowing you to adjust how your events are organized as your needs change.

<DotDivider />

## Apple Watch integration
The Profile page also includes options related to your Apple Watch. From here, you can add the Lystaria watch complication, giving you quick access to key features directly from your watch face.

<img src="/img/screenshots/profile/view2.png" class="docs-image-single"/>

This extends the app beyond your phone and allows for faster interaction when you need it.

<DotDivider />

## Onboarding and guidance
If you ever want a refresher on how the app works, you can rerun the onboarding tour from the Profile page. This will guide you through key features and explain the purpose of different parts of the app again.

<img src="/img/screenshots/profile/view3.png" class="docs-image-single"/>

You can also view the welcome screen at any time. This takes you back to the beginning of the experience, allowing you to revisit the introduction and flow of the app whenever you need.

<GradientDivider />

The Profile page brings together personalization, system settings, and connected features into one place. By centralizing these controls, Lystaria makes it easier to adjust your experience without interrupting how you use the rest of the app.

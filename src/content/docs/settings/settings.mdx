## Managing your calendars
One of the core functions of the Settings page is managing your calendars. Calendars are what allow your events to stay organized, and Settings gives you control over how they look and how they are labeled.

<img src="/img/screenshots/settings/editcal.png" class="docs-image-single"/>

You can edit both the name and the color of your calendars. This gives you flexibility to adjust your system over time as your needs change. Whether you are refining categories or simply want better visual clarity, these options allow your calendar system to stay aligned with how you think and organize.

<DotDivider />

## Feature tour
The Settings page also allows you to rerun the feature tour. By toggling this on, the app will walk you through the interface again, explaining the header icon buttons and what each of them is used for.

This is especially helpful if you have forgotten what certain buttons do or if you want to re-familiarize yourself with the layout. It can also be useful after updates or changes, giving you a quick way to reconnect with how everything is structured without needing to guess or explore blindly.

<DotDivider />

## Calendar sync
Another important part of Settings is calendar sync. You can enable syncing between Lystaria and your iPhone calendar directly from this page.

The sync is two-way, which means changes can move in both directions. When you turn on sync, you will be able to choose which calendar you want to sync from and which calendar you want to sync to. This gives you control over how your external calendar and your Lystaria events interact, rather than forcing everything into a single default connection.

This feature is especially useful if you already rely on your device’s calendar but want to integrate it into your Lystaria system. It allows your events to exist in both places while still maintaining structure and organization.

<DotDivider />

## Reopening the welcome experience
At the bottom of the Settings page, you have the option to view the welcome screen again. Selecting this will take you back to the first screen of the onboarding experience, allowing you to go through it from the beginning.

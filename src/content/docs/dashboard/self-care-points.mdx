## Main Point Cards
At the top of the page, there are four main cards that show your current points, current level, total points today, and lifetime points.

<div class="docs-image-row">
<img src="/img/screenshots/selfcare/view1.png" class="docs-image-single"/>
<img src="/img/screenshots/selfcare/view2.png" class="docs-image-single"/>
</div>

These cards give you a quick snapshot of where you are right now. You can immediately see what you have earned today, how many points you have overall, and what level you are currently on.

<DotDivider />

## Level Progress
The Level Progress card shows how close you are to reaching your next level. The progress bar fills based on where you are in your current level.

Each level is 100 points. For example, if the card shows 42 / 100, that means you have 58 points left before reaching the next level. This makes your progress easy to understand at a glance.

<DotDivider />

## Points Breakdown
The Points Breakdown card gives you a more detailed view of your point totals.

It shows your current points, lifetime points, spent points, and points earned today. This helps separate your overall progress from what you currently have available and what you have already used.

<DotDivider />

## Recent Activity
The Recent Activity card shows what you have done across the app to earn points. This includes activity from different systems in Lystaria, allowing you to see where your points are coming from.

Up to four activity cards are displayed at a time. If you have more entries, you can use the load more button to reveal additional activity.

<DotDivider />

## How to Earn Points
The How to Earn card explains which actions can earn points and how many points each system gives.

<img src="/img/screenshots/selfcare/view3.png" class="docs-image-single"/>

This helps you understand how your daily actions connect to your point progress. It also gives you a quick reference for which parts of the app contribute to your Self Care Points.

<DotDivider />

## Weekly History
The bottom section of the page is where you can view and log your history.

<div class="docs-image-row">
<img src="/img/screenshots/selfcare/history1.png" class="docs-image-single"/>
<img src="/img/screenshots/selfcare/history2.png" class="docs-image-single"/>
</div>

History is saved automatically right before the weekly reset every Monday at 12:00 AM. You can tap into a week to see the full history for that period, including the level you reached and the points you accumulated.

You can also manually log your history using the Log History button if you want to save a snapshot yourself.

<GradientDivider />

The Self Care Points page is designed to make your progress visible. By tracking points, levels, recent activity, earning methods, and weekly history, it helps you see how your actions across Lystaria build into something meaningful over time.

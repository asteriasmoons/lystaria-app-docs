## Bubble Distraction
The first card on the Toolbox page is the Bubble Distraction tool.

This tool opens a calming page filled with floating bubbles. You can tap the bubbles to pop them, giving your mind and hands something simple to focus on when everything feels too loud, too much, or too scattered.

<img src="/img/screenshots/toolbox/view1.png" class="docs-image-single"/>

You can stay on this page for as long as you need. There is no pressure to finish quickly, complete a task, or do anything perfectly. The purpose is simply to give yourself a soft distraction that helps your nervous system settle a little more.

<DotDivider />

## Meditation Timer
The Meditation Timer card gives you a visual countdown for meditation, stillness, rest, or quiet focus.

<img src="/img/screenshots/toolbox/view2.png" class="docs-image-single"/>

The timer uses a gradient circle ring that slowly drains of color as your session counts down. This gives you a gentle visual way to understand how much time is left without needing to stare at numbers the entire time.

You can choose from preset times or set a custom time that fits what you need in the moment. Once the timer ends, a soft tune will play to let you know your session is complete.

<DotDivider />

## Breathing Timer
The Breathing Timer is for guided breathing sessions when you need help coming back to center.

<img src="/img/screenshots/toolbox/view3.png" class="docs-image-single"/>

This tool gives you a simple breathing exercise experience that you can follow for as long as you need. It is meant to help slow your attention, reconnect with your body, and give you something steady to focus on when your thoughts feel rushed or overwhelming.

For now, there is no strict session length. You can use the Breathing Timer until you feel ready to stop.

<DotDivider />

## Burn Box
The Burn Box is a release tool for negative, intrusive, or taunting thoughts.

You type in the thought that keeps bothering you, then watch as a flame burns it away. This tool is meant to create a small symbolic release moment, giving you a visual way to let the thought leave your mind instead of continuing to circle inside it.

Burn Box does not force you to argue with the thought or solve it perfectly. It simply gives you a place to name what is bothering you and watch it disappear.

<DotDivider />

## Future Toolbox Additions
More tools will be added to the Toolbox page in the future, but each new feature needs to make sense for the purpose of this space.

Toolbox is not meant to become a random collection of generic wellness tools. Each addition should support grounding, emotional regulation, sensory relief, self-trust, or mental reset in a way that feels meaningful and useful.

You can stay updated on future Toolbox additions through the upcoming release notes feature in the app.

The Toolbox page is designed to help you return to yourself when things feel overwhelming. Through sensory distraction, meditation, guided breathing, and symbolic release, it gives you small but meaningful ways to pause, reset, and move through difficult moments with more support.

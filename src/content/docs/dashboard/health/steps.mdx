## Daily Step Tracking
The main focus of the Steps page is today’s steps card. This card shows a progress ring filled with a gradient that reflects how many steps you have completed for the day.

Inside the progress ring, you will see your current step count displayed clearly. This gives you an immediate understanding of your activity level without needing to look anywhere else.

If you have set a goal, it will also be shown on the card so you can compare your current steps against your target.

<DotDivider />

## Controls and Actions
Below the progress ring are a few simple controls to help you manage your step data.

The Goal button allows you to set or update your daily step goal. The Refresh button pulls in any new data, which is especially useful since step tracking happens outside the app through your device or Apple Health.

There is also a Today button, which is useful when navigating through your history. If you are viewing another date, tapping this button will bring you back to the current day.

<DotDivider />

## Goal Calendar and History
The goal calendar works similarly to the one in the Water Tracking page.

You can navigate through different months using the left and right chevron buttons. Each day is represented by a bubble, and tapping on any date will update the steps card above with that day’s data.

This allows you to easily look back at your activity and understand how consistent you have been over time.

<GradientDivider />

The Steps system in Lystaria is designed to stay straightforward while still giving you meaningful insight into your daily movement. It keeps everything visible, easy to access, and connected to your overall health tracking experience.

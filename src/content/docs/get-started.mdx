## What Lystaria is

Lystaria is an iOS app that brings together planning, reminders, habits,
journaling, reading, and personal support tools in one connected space. It is
not meant to feel cold or mechanical. It is meant to feel like a place you can
return to for clarity, rhythm, and care.

Instead of splitting your life across a dozen disconnected systems, Lystaria
tries to let those pieces live together in a way that feels natural.

## Other features worth exploring
Lystaria also includes a range of additional features that support everyday life, emotional care, health tracking, and organization. While some of them are more lightweight, they still play an important role in helping the app feel more complete and supportive.

### Mood Logging
Track your moods over time and build a clearer understanding of your emotional patterns. This feature helps you reflect, notice shifts, and keep a record of your emotional life in a more organized way.

### Notes
Use Notes to store quick thoughts, ideas, reminders, fragments, or anything else you want to keep close without turning it into a full journal entry. It is a simpler space for things that still matter.

### Bookmarks
Save and organize links you want to revisit later. The Bookmarks feature gives you a dedicated place for useful resources, articles, websites, and references so they do not get lost.

### Checklists
Create and manage lists for tasks, routines, or anything else you want to break into smaller pieces. Checklists are especially helpful when you want structure in a lightweight and flexible format.

### Toolbox
The Toolbox is designed to support your mental wellness in the moment. It offers a place for grounding and supportive tools when you need calm, regulation, or a gentler reset.

### Health
The Health page helps you log personal metrics and exercise in one organized place. It is there to make important physical information easier to track and revisit over time.

### Medication Manager
Use the Medication Manager to keep your medications organized in one dedicated space. This feature helps support consistency, clarity, and a better understanding of your routines.

### Symptom Logger
The Symptom Logger gives you space to record what you are experiencing over time. This can help you notice patterns, track changes, and keep a more useful history of your health experiences.

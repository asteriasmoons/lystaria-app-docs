# Get Started

Welcome to the Lystaria documentation.

This space is here to help you understand the app, explore its systems, and feel more at home inside everything Lystaria offers. Whether you are just getting oriented or trying to understand a specific feature, these docs are meant to guide you clearly without feeling dry or overwhelming.

## What Lystaria is

Lystaria is a personal iOS app designed to support everyday life through a blend of structure, reflection, and softness. It brings together tools for planning, tracking, journaling, reminders, habits, reading, and emotional support in one connected space.

Instead of feeling like a cold productivity tool, Lystaria is meant to feel more like a personal world you can return to.

## What these docs are for

These docs will help you:

- understand what each feature does
- learn how different systems connect together
- get familiar with the overall flow of the app
- find answers more quickly when something feels unclear

## Where to begin

If you are new to the app, these sections are a good place to start:

- **Reminders** for scheduling and recurring structure
- **Habits** for consistency and daily rhythm
- **Journaling** for books, entries, and reflection
- **Calendar** for events and planning
- **Reading** for books, sessions, and progress tracking

## A simple way to use this site

A good approach is:

1. start with the feature you use most
2. read the overview for that section
3. move into the smaller details only when you need them

You do not need to read everything in order. These docs are here to support you, not give you homework.

## What Lystaria is

Lystaria is an iOS app that brings together planning, reminders, habits,
journaling, reading, and personal support tools in one connected space. It is
not meant to feel cold or mechanical. It is meant to feel like a place you can
return to for clarity, rhythm, and care.

Instead of splitting your life across a dozen disconnected systems, Lystaria
tries to let those pieces live together in a way that feels natural.

## What these docs are for

These docs are here to help you:

- understand what each feature does
- learn how the app’s systems connect together
- figure out where to start
- answer specific questions quickly when something feels unclear

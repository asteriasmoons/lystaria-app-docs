## Moon Phase Tracking
The first card on the Dashboard is the moon phase tracking card. This card is live and updates continuously, giving you an always-current view of the moon’s state.

<div class="docs-image-row">
<img src="/img/screenshots/dashboard/view1.png" class="docs-image-single"/>
<img src="/img/screenshots/dashboard/view6.png" class="docs-image-single"/>
</div>

It shows a moon phase icon along with the phase name, a zodiac icon with the zodiac sign, the moon day within the current lunar cycle, and the illumination percentage. This makes it more than a decorative feature. It gives you a quick way to stay aware of the current lunar energy and where you are in the cycle.

When you tap the card, it opens a popup with more detailed moon-related information. There you can see the general vibe of the phase, a fuller description, ritual suggestions, and what that phase is considered the best time for.

## Daily Momentum
The Daily Momentum card gives you a visual snapshot of how many parts of your life you have touched in a single day. It uses a grid of six areas: Journal, Mood, Habits, Water Tracking, Step Counting, and Reading.

Each square fills with a gradient once that area has been completed or engaged with for the day. This creates an immediate sense of how active and connected your day has been across multiple areas rather than focusing on only one thing.

At the bottom of the card, your activation state is shown based on how many of these life areas you have touched that day. This gives the card a little more meaning than a simple checklist, because it helps you understand how fully engaged you have been with your daily systems overall.

<DotDivider />

## Self Care Points Preview
Below that, you get a quick-glance view of your Self Care Points and current level. This gives you immediate feedback on your progress without needing to leave the Dashboard.

<img src="/img/screenshots/dashboard/view2.png" class="docs-image-single"/>

If you want more detail, you can navigate to the Dashboard header and tap the second icon button to go to the full Self Care Points page. That deeper page gives you a more complete picture of your level, your progress, and your point history, while the Dashboard card keeps the essential information visible at a glance.

<DotDivider />

## Active Streaks
The Active Streaks card shows your current active streaks across the app. Instead of looking at one feature in isolation, it gives you a broader sense of what you are maintaining consistently.

This is useful because streaks are often one of the clearest ways to understand momentum. Seeing them gathered together in one place helps you recognize where you are being consistent and where your routines are holding steady.

<DotDivider />

## Daily Balance
The Daily Balance card shows your progress in different life areas and how much of each area you have completed. Each section includes a progress bar along with a visible percentage, making it easy to understand how balanced or unbalanced your day currently feels.

<div class="docs-image-row">
<img src="/img/screenshots/dashboard/view2.png" class="docs-image-single"/>
<img src="/img/screenshots/dashboard/view3.png" class="docs-image-single"/>
</div>

The card also tells you which features are connected to each life area. That added context helps you understand how the app is grouping things behind the scenes and what actions contribute to each category. This turns the card into something more reflective than a simple progress display, because it helps you see not just what you have done, but what parts of life are actually being supported.

<DotDivider />

## Daily Tarot Tip
The daily tarot tip card gives you a single tarot card for the day. Once you draw your card, it stays the same until midnight in your local time.

This gives the card a sense of continuity throughout the day. Rather than constantly changing, it acts as a stable daily theme or point of reflection that you can return to whenever you want.

<DotDivider />

## Daily Lenormand Card
The daily lenormand card works similarly, but offers a more simple and direct interpretation of the day. Once you draw it, that card remains in place for the rest of the day.

<img src="/img/screenshots/dashboard/view3.png" class="docs-image-single"/>

This gives you another form of daily guidance that is often more compact and straightforward than tarot. It complements the tarot card rather than replacing it, giving you a different style of symbolic reflection.

<DotDivider />

## Daily Horoscope
The daily horoscope card gives you another layer of guidance. The first tab provides your daily horoscope reading, while the Explore tab allows you to look into other zodiac signs as well.

You can clear and explore as many times as you want if you want to browse different signs and readings. This makes the horoscope card feel more interactive and exploratory rather than fixed to only one small reading.

<DotDivider />

## Consistency Card
The Consistency Card is there to help you understand your strongest and weakest areas over time. It highlights your Most Consistent area of life, the area that Needs Attention, your Strongest Streak, and the area that has been Least Active this week.

<img src="/img/screenshots/dashboard/view4.png" class="docs-image-single"/>

This makes it especially useful when you want to know where your effort is naturally going and where things may be slipping. Rather than leaving you to guess, the card reflects those patterns back to you so you can respond more intentionally.

<DotDivider />

## Wellness Wall
The Wellness Wall card helps you understand which parts of your life are actually influencing your well-being. It looks at your life scores across areas like journaling, water tracking, step count, and habits, then shows meaningful insight statements based on your data.

<img src="/img/screenshots/dashboard/view5.png" class="docs-image-single"/>

For example, it may tell you something like, "Your mood averages 3.7 / 5 on days you journal." This makes the Wellness Wall one of the most personally revealing cards on the Dashboard, because it helps you see what genuinely supports you rather than what you simply assume should.

<GradientDivider />

The Dashboard in Lystaria is where the app becomes a connected system rather than a collection of separate tools. By bringing together live lunar data, daily progress, self-care growth, streaks, wellness insights, and spiritual guidance, it gives you a fuller understanding of how your life is moving as a whole.

Instead of checking ten different places to understand your day, the Dashboard helps you see the bigger picture in one space. It reflects your progress, your patterns, and your energy back to you in a way that feels both practical and personal.
